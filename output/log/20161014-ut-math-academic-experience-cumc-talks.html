20161014 UT Math Academic Experience: CUMC Talks

15 Oct 2016

Team,

I am very grateful to all of you who have come today for a series of fascinating talks by Zachary Karry, Angela Wu and Calder Morton-Ferguson (by the way, today, Saturday 15, is Calder's birthday).

From the Leech lattice, Hadwider-Nelson and beyond, there is a trove of known and unknown results awaiting our attention.

I wish you a restful and productive weekend.
