Knowledge is power. The ability to learn, create and share knowledge is the ability all of us have.

But using this ability requires deliberate practice and courage.

umu, which stands for universal math umu and is read as $$u$$-$$\mu$$, is the framework to facilitate the progress of mathematics.

Through discourse, exposition, organisation of math circles and seminars, problem-solving and independent research, umu strives to improve the mathematical culture and foster creative boldness.
